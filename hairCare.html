Dry and Damaged Hair

To repair dry and damaged hair, use any of these listed household items to smoothen hair locks.

Avocado
Pit an avocado and mix with one egg in a bowl. Then, rinse hair and massage the mix in. Leave the mixture in hair for at least 20 minutes. Repeat once a week for damaged hair and once a month once hair is healthy.

Olive Oil
Warm a Â½ cup of olive oil without boiling and massage into hair. Wrap hair in a plastic bag and towel to keep moisture in. After 45 minutes, shampoo hair and rinse completely.

Humidifier
Using a humidifier at night, especially during the winter allows the air to fill with moisture so hair will not be as dry.

Oily and Greasy Hair


Aloe Vera
Use a cup of mild shampoo, a tablespoon of lemon juice and a teaspoon of aloe vera gel. Chose either fresh aloe vera or aloe vera gel in this mix. Use the mixture daily and leave the remaining mixture in the fridge.

Baby Powder
A good alternate for dry shampoo is baby powder. Sprinkle baby powder into hair and brush through for quick oil free hair.

Diluted Vinegar and Lemon Juice
After shampooing, rinse hair with either 2 tablespoons of white vinegar or lemon juice and a cup of water. Then, rinse mixture out with plain water.

Mint
Boil mint leaves with 2 cups of water for 15 to 20 minutes and use the mint water with shampoo to help reduce greasiness.

Egg Yolk and Lemon Juice
Crack 2 eggs and add a few lemon juice drops. Mix, apply to wet hair, leave mixture for five to seven minutes and finally rinse out of hair.
