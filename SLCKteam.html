About Us

Our team, SLCK, was created through the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program where we were taught how to code. Members Sara, Lauren, Cindy and Karis (SLCK) make up the team. For our final project, we decided to develop a website that gives information we find useful about home remedies. This project was great practice to test our problem solving skills and basic coding knowledge.

The name Ambrosia was derived from the food for the Greek gods. According to mythology, it can purify oneself and grant longevity. This is similar to the goals of the remedies we list on the website.
