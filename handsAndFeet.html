Grow Nails Faster

Long and healthy nails are visually appealing to many people, but the wait for them to grow is aggravating. To speeden the nail growing process, use orange juice to soak nails for at least 10 minutes and rinse. Repeat daily until satisfied.

Yellow Nails

To reduce nail yellowness, chose one of these methods:
  1. Soak nails in a bowl of lemon juice for 10-15 minutes daily until yellowness disappears.
  2. Soak nails in a water and hydrogen peroxide mix for a few minutes and then rinse with water.



Yellow nails may be caused due to a poor diet. To improve nail health, take initiative to eat foods that are high in nutrients.

Dry/Rough Hands and Feet

The fats in many of these treatments allow hands and feet to become soft.
To get rid of dry and roughness use:

Olive Oil
  1. Use warm olive oil and massage for 5 to 10 minutes daily.
    2. OR
  2. Use sugar and olive oil to scrub hands and feet. Rinse off after 5 minutes. Repeat twice a week.


Coconut Oil
Massage extra-virgin coconut oil for 5 minutes onto hands and feet before sleeping. Wear gloves or plastic bags to leave on overnight for greater results. Repeat daily.

Oatmeal
Mix two tablespoons of ground oatmeal, 1 teaspoon of honey and a few drops of water. Then, rub onto hands and feet. Rinse off in 10 minutes. Repeat once a week.

Milk Cream
Rub fresh milk cream onto hands and feet. Rinse after 10 minutes. Repeat daily.

Honey
Massage honey onto hands and feet. Rinse after 10 minutes.

Aloe Vera
Use fresh aloe vera or gel to massage hands and feet. Wash off after 10 to 15 minutes. Repeat 1-2 times a day.
