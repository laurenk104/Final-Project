Ginger Tea: Chinese and Indian medicine

Benefits:
Immune system booster
Warms up the body
Remove toxins from organs
Prevents blood clot
Contain gingerol (oily resin that acts as antioxidant anti-inflammatory agent)

How to make:
Serving: 1
Ginger
Water
Small pot or saucepan
Honey

Cut 4-5 ginger slices into small cubes.
Submerge into 2 cups of water.
Set stove fire on high until boil.
Lower fire until liquid turns dark and strongly smells like ginger.
Strain into mug and add desired amount of honey.

Helpful for:
Nausea
Gastrointestinal issues
Ulcer
Digestion
Immunity
Stomach ache
Morning sickness
Vomiting
