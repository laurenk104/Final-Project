Ginger Tea

Origin: China and India

Benefits:

- Immune System booster
- Warms up the body
- Removes toxins from organs
- Prevents blood clots
- Contain gingerol (oily resin that acts as antioxidant anti-inflammatory agent)

How to Make:

  1. Cut 4-5 ginger slices into small cubes or cubed strips.

  2. Submerge slices into 2 cups of water.

  3. Set stove fire on high until boil.

  4. Lower fire until liquid turns dark and strongly smells like ginger.

  5. Strain into mug and add desired amount of honey.

Helpful for:

- Nausea
- Gastrointestinal Issues
- Ulcer
- Digestion
- Immunity
- Stomachache
- Motion Sickness
- Vomiting

Lemon Balm Tea


European tea that clears the mind and soothes the mood


Benefits:


- Reduce fine lines and wrinkles
- Promote calm and positive mood
- Alerts and Sharpen the mind
- Contain antioxidant to eliminate free radicals and supports aging brain cells
- Improve brain cell function: memory, mood, thoughts
- Maintain Blood Glucose
- Protect liver from harmful effects of unhealthy habits
- Improve sleep quality


Helpful For:


- Stress managing
- Skincare
- Brain Function
- Liver
- Insomnia
- Anxiety
- Stress Reliever


How to Prepare:


Follow instructions on tea bag or box


OR


Serving: 4 cups


Lazy Way:
  1. Take 6-10 cups lemon balm leaves and stuff into tea any container.

  2. Add boiling water and wait for 20 minutes.

More Work:
  1. Rinse and pat dry about 6-10 cups of lemon balm leaves.

  2. Hang leaves outside in the sun and wait until they wrinkle and turn darker. OR Preheat oven and arrange leaves on paper towel. Layer towels on baking tray and place in oven. Wait for more than 20 minutes, preferably overnight.

  3. Crumble up leaves and store them until you want to seep leaves in boiling water and drink tea!

Rosehip Tea


What is it? Rosehip is the fruit of a rose. The hip is what is left after flower has bloomed and petals fallen.
Other names: rose hep, rose haw


Benefits:


- High levels of Vitamin A:
  • Regenerates skin and prevents wrinkles

  • Fights infections (benefits immune system)

- Keeps skin elastic and moisturized
- Contains many antioxidants that can prevent heart muscle disease
- Fight bacterial infections

Helpful For:


- Aging skin
- Arthritis
- Kidney Disorder
- Consipation

Tips:


- Contains vitamin C! (50% more than oranges!)
- Do not store in metal container! The acid in fruit doesn’t go well with metal!
- Can freeze or dry the flowers. Could be made into jelly or added to sauces.
