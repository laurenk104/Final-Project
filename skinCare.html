Glowing Skin

To get glowing skin, use one of the listed treatments below.

Turmeric

Mix 1/2-1 teaspoon of turmeric powder and 4 tablespoons of gram flour(chickpea flour). Then, add either enough milk or water to the mixture so it can become a paste. Apply the paste to face or body and rinse after 15 minutes with water.

Baking Soda

Put a mixture of 1 teaspoon of baking soda, 1 teaspoon of extra virgin olive oil and 1/2 teaspoon honey onto damp face or body. Leave for 10 minutes, rinse and then moisturize.

Lemon Scrub

Scrub a mixture of 2 teaspoons of lemon juice and 2 teaspoons of sugar onto face. Rinse off and repeat twice a week.

Papaya

Cucumber

Apply a thin layer of grated cucumber and yogurt onto skin and rinse off once dried. Repeat every three to four days.

Dry Skin

To get rid of dry skin, try one of the treatments below.

Olive Oil

Place a thin layer of extra virgin olive oil onto skin and then apply regular moisturizer over it. Massage olive oil onto dry areas. Wait 30 minutes and then rinse off in shower. Finally, moisturize. Olive Oil Scrub: Create a mixture of two tablespoons of olive oil, four tablespoons of fine brown sugar, and one tablespoon of honey. Rub onto dry areas and then rinse off.

Milk Cream

Before showering rub a mixture of a few drops of lemon juice, one teaspoon of milk and two teaspoons of milk cream. Repeat daily. Create a paste with milk cream and gram flour. Leave on face for 15 minutes and then rinse it. Repeat daily.

Milk

Soak washcloth with cold milk and place on dry area for five minutes. Wash off milk with warm water and moisturize with lotion after. Repeat every other day. Create a mixture of 4 tablespoons of milk and a few drops of rose water to rub on dry areas. Rinse with cold water and repeat twice daily.

Honey

Rub honey on dry areas and leave it on for five to 10 minutes. Then rinse off and repeat daily. Heat equal parts of unprocessed honey, beeswax, and olive oil. Remove from heat and mix in the honey and olive oil. Apply to dry areas and leave on for 10 minutes. Rinse and repeat daily.
